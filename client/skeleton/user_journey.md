### USER JOURNEY
A user arrives at the Random Quest website and is greeted with a login and registration page. From here, their journey splits depending on whether they're new or returning.
If they're new, they fill out a quick registration form with their name, email, and password. After confirming their email, they're redirected back to the login page where they sign in with their new credentials. From there, they're walked through a category selection screen where they pick their preferences. Once that's done, a welcome message greets them before they land on their dashboard for the very first time, where their first challenge is waiting.
If they're a returning user, it's much simpler. They sign in and land straight on their dashboard where a fresh challenge has already been generated based on their saved preferences.
Once on the dashboard, the user has full control over their experience. They can complete a challenge and earn 10 points toward their total, save a challenge they like so they can come back to it later, browse through their challenge history, update their category preferences at any time, or visit their profile to update their name, email, or password.
As points stack up from completed challenges, users climb the leaderboard. The top 50 players are ranked among three tiers — those in spots 26 through 50 hold the title of Quest Squire, players in spots 11 through 25 are known as Quest Knights, and the elite top 10 carry the title of Crimson Quest Master.
